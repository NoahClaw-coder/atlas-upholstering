Dining Chair Reupholstering

Revive your dining set — smarter than buying new

Dining chairs take a beating. Daily use, spills, pets, children — over time, even a quality set starts to look worn, faded, or simply dated. But if the frames are solid, replacing your chairs is a waste. Reupholstering gives you a brand-new-looking set at a fraction of the replacement cost — and unlike mass-market furniture, the result is built to your standard.

At Atlas Upholstering, we reupholster dining sets of all sizes — from intimate 4-chair tables to formal 12-chair dining rooms. Every chair in the set is done at the same time, with the same fabric and the same foam, so the result is perfectly consistent across the entire set.

Why Reupholster?

3 reasons to reupholster instead of replace

Cost-Effective

Reupholstering a solid dining set typically costs significantly less than a comparable new set of the same quality. If your frames are good, you're simply not getting value by replacing them — you're paying for wood and construction you already own.

Consistent Set

Every chair in your set is reupholstered at the same time with the same material, so the colour, texture, and finish are perfectly uniform across the entire table. No mismatched lots, no discontinuations — just a set that looks like it belongs together.

Your Fabric Choice

With 200+ fabric options, you're not limited to what a retailer happens to stock. Choose a performance weave that wipes clean in seconds, a velvet that photographs beautifully, or bring in your own fabric. The result is exactly the chair you want.

How It Works

Simple, fast, painless

1. Measure & Assess

Bring your chairs in or send us photos. We assess the frame condition, measure the seat and back panels, and confirm whether foam replacement is needed. You'll receive a per-chair and full-set quote with no obligation.

2. Fabric Selection

Choose from our curated selection of dining-appropriate fabrics — performance weaves, easy-clean vinyls, rich velvets, and more. We help you select a material that works with your dining room's palette and stands up to real-life use.

3. Expert Reupholstering

Your chairs are stripped, the foam refreshed where needed, and the new fabric applied with precision. Corners are pulled tight, seams are clean, and every chair in the set is finished to the same standard. Ready for pickup or delivery within 1–2 weeks.
