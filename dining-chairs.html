Dining Chairs

Expert Dining Chair Reupholstery

Worn dining chairs bring down the look of the whole room. Atlas reupholsters dining chairs of any style — traditional carved frames, mid-century designs, modern parsons chairs, and everything in between. We match fabric across a full set so every chair looks perfectly consistent.

Drop-in seats, full-back chairs, bench seats, and upholstered arm chairs — we handle all types. Foam is replaced as needed. We can also refinish wooden frames at the same time for a complete refresh.

Sets of 4, 6, 8, or more — matched consistently
Drop-in seat pads, full-back, and parsons-style chairs
Foam replacement for worn or flat seat cushions
Wide fabric selection: leather, velvet, boucle, performance
Piping, nailhead trim, and decorative finish options
Frame repair and wood refinishing available
