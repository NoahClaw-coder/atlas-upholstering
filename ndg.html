Serving NDG

Montreal's upholstery studio — in the heart of NDG

Atlas Upholstering has been a fixture of Notre-Dame-de-Grâce since 1967. Our studio sits at 2535 Boul. Cavendish — a short walk or drive from anywhere in NDG. For over five decades, neighbours from Monkland Village to Côte-Saint-Luc Road have brought us their sofas, armchairs, dining sets, and heirloom pieces, trusting us to restore them to their very best.

NDG is a neighbourhood that values quality and craftsmanship — and so do we. Whether you have a single dining chair that needs new fabric, a sectional sofa that's seen better days, or a vintage armchair you'd like to transform completely, our team brings the same meticulous attention to every piece that walks through our door. We work with an extensive selection of over 200 fabrics, from premium leathers and performance velvets to natural linens and durable outdoor textiles.
