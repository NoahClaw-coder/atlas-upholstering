Our Story

Three decades of Montreal craftsmanship

Atlas Upholstering was founded on a simple belief: that quality furniture deserves a second life, and that the craft of upholstery — when done well — is as much art as it is trade. From a small workshop on Cavendish, we've spent over 30 years earning the trust of Montreal homeowners, interior designers, restaurateurs, and hotels.

We've restored Victorian antiques, rebuilt commercial banquettes for some of the city's most beloved restaurants, and crafted custom headboards that anchor entire bedroom designs. Each project is different. Each one gets the same unwavering attention to detail.

We're proud to be part of Montreal's fabric — bilingual, rooted in craft, and committed to the longevity of the beautiful things that fill people's homes and lives.

What We Stand For

Our values guide every project

🎯

Meticulous Precision

Every cut, every seam, every stitch matters. We measure twice, cut once, and take pride in work that meets an exacting standard — even in details no one else will see.

🤝

Honest Communication

We tell you what your piece needs, what it will cost, and how long it will take — before we begin. No surprises, no hidden fees, no vague timelines.

🌱

Sustainability

Reupholstering is inherently sustainable. We extend the life of furniture by decades, reduce waste, and help clients make environmentally responsible choices without sacrificing beauty.

🏙️

Community Roots

We are a Montreal business serving Montreal people. We speak both official languages, support local suppliers where possible, and take pride in contributing to this city's rich creative culture.
