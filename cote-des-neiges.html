Serving Côte-des-Neiges

Côte-des-Neiges residents trust Atlas Upholstering for all their upholstery needs. Our studio at 2535 Boul. Cavendish is just minutes away from CDN — easily accessible by car or public transit. For over 50 years, we've served the diverse and dynamic community of Côte-des-Neiges, helping families and businesses keep their furniture looking its best.
