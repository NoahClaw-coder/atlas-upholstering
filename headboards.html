Headboards

Custom & Reupholstered Headboards

A beautifully upholstered headboard is one of the most impactful upgrades you can make to a bedroom. Atlas builds headboards from scratch to your exact dimensions, or reupholsters your existing headboard in a new fabric — tufted, panelled, arched, or flat, in virtually any fabric you choose.

We work with velvet, linen, leather, boucle, and performance fabrics. All headboards are built on solid hardwood frames with high-density foam padding. Twin, double, queen, and king sizes — or completely custom dimensions.

Twin, double, queen, king & custom sizes
Button tufting, channel tufting, and flat panel styles
Arched, rectangular, and custom silhouettes
Wall-mounted and standard floor-standing options
Matching footboards and bed frames available
Reupholstering of existing headboards
