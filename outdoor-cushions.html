Outdoor Cushions

Custom Patio & Deck Cushions

Don't throw out your patio furniture because the cushions have faded or worn out. Atlas makes custom outdoor cushions to fit your existing frames exactly — in premium outdoor fabrics that resist UV, moisture, and Montreal's demanding climate.

We fabricate seat cushions, back cushions, and full chair pads for all brands of outdoor furniture. Bring us your old cushion or frame dimensions and we'll cut, fill, and finish new cushions that fit and look like new.

Seat cushions, back cushions, and full chair pads
UV-resistant, water-repellent outdoor fabrics
Quick-dry foam cores that won't retain moisture
Custom sizes to fit any brand of patio furniture
Zipper enclosures for easy seasonal cleaning
Matching sets for sofas, chairs, and ottomans
