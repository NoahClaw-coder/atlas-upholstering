Serving the South Shore

The South Shore's trusted upholstery studio — just across the bridge

Clients from the South Shore — Longueuil, Brossard, Saint-Lambert and beyond — have trusted Atlas Upholstering for over 50 years. Our Montreal studio at 2535 Boul. Cavendish is easily accessible from the South Shore via the Champlain or Victoria bridges, and we offer full pickup and delivery service so the drive is never a barrier to getting exceptional upholstery work done.

South Shore clients choose us for the quality of our work and the breadth of our expertise. From the classic Victorian sofas of Saint-Lambert's older homes to contemporary sectionals in Brossard's newest developments, we have the skills and materials to handle any project beautifully. Our over 200 fabric options include everything from easy-clean performance fabrics perfect for family homes, to lush velvets and buttery leathers for a more refined look.
